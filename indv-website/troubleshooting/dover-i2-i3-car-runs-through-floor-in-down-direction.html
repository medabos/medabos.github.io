Dover I-2 / I-3 Car runs through floor in DOWN Direction

  • Plugged down filter screen.The filter screen is located behind the down control block. If debris is clogging the filter screen, try to identify what the debris is so that you can take measures to eliminate what is producing the debris. i.e. Muffler material, dirt/sand, rust, lint, etc.
  • Down Stop Adjuster is IN too far.
  • Down Slowdown Adjuster is IN too far.
  • Down Leveling Speed is too fast.
  • Electrical problems.Check the Down Slow Solenoid to see if it remains energized at or below floor level. Use a screwdriver or other steel tool to feel for the magnetism of the solenoid coil.

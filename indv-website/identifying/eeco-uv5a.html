EECO UV-5A

These valves are a identified by pipe size. 1/2", 3/4", 1", 1/ 1/4", 1 1/2". The first number in the serial number indicate what this size is. Note: In some valves the down and up flow rates are different. On these valves there will be an extra digit in the serial number prefix.


UV-5A 1/2"

  • Serial number prefix: 3
  • Flow rate: 10 GPM to 80 GPM

UV-5A 3/4"

  • Serial number prefix: 4
  • Flow Rate: 35 GPM to 150 GPM

UV-5A 1"

  • Serial number prefix: 5
  • Flow Rate: 55 GPM to 200 GPM

UV-5A 1 1/4"

  • Serial number prefix: 6
  • Flow Rate: 80 GPM to 200+ GPM

UV-5A 1 1/2"

  • Serial number prefix: 7
  • Flow Rate: 105 GPM to 200+ GPM

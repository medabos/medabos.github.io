Dover I-2 / I-3 Leak Down Problems

  • Down solenoids are damaged or contain debris. You can check for a leak down in the solenoid tubes by turning the down start adjuster all the way in. Count your turns, so that you can return it to the orginal setting. If this reduces the leak down, the one or both of your solenoids are the problem. Replace solenoid assembly.
  • Down and/or Check Piston has damage or debris in seating surfaces. To check remove 6 bolts on right side of valve. (Same side as the down main speed / leveling adjuster) Carefully remove, there are 2 springs under plate. Pull pistons out and inspect for damage or debris on seating surface. If there is no damage, clean with lint-free cloth and reassemble. If damaged, clean oil of the contaminants that damaged the valve and replace valve.

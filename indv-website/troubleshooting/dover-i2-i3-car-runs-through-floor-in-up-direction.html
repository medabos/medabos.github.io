Dover I-2 / I-3 Car runs through floor in UP Direction

  • Low Pressure Adjuster (Bypass) is IN too far.
  • UP Stop Adjuster is IN too far.
  • UP Slowdown Adjuster is IN too far.
  • UP Leveling Speed is too fast.
  • UP high speed is too fast.Not enough room for slowdown.
  • UP fast solenoid is not closing quick enough.This could be due to a debris or a broken spring.
  • Electrical Issues.This could be due to the UP slow solenoid coil turning off too late. Use a screwdriver or other steel tool to feel for the magnetism of the solenoid coil.

EECO UV-7B

Although there is a prefix number that indicates the flow rate of the valve, they are sometimes different then the factory settings. This difference is due to the unique piston design. The down and the bypass pistons can be adjusted to have diffent flow rates in the field.


UV-7B

  • Serial number prefixs: 5, 7, 9, E, T, S
  • Flow rate: 150 GPM to 400+ GPM
